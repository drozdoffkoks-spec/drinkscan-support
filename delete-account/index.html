Delete your DrinkScan account and data

Use this page to delete your DrinkScan account and request deletion of associated app data.

Support home Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open the DrinkScan app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Choose Delete account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

Request deletion by email

You can also request account and data deletion by emailing drinkscan.support@gmail.com.

Please email us from the address connected to your DrinkScan account, or include enough information for us to verify account ownership.

What data is deleted

  • Your DrinkScan account record and authentication profile.
  • Your saved scan history associated with the account.
  • Account-linked AI usage and scan quota records.
  • Account-linked preferences stored in DrinkScan systems.

Guest mode local data

If you use DrinkScan in guest mode, your guest scan history is stored locally on your device and is not linked to an online DrinkScan account. You can remove guest data by deleting the app or clearing the app data on your device.

Community contributed photos

If you opted in to contribute high-confidence bottle photos to the DrinkScan community photo pool, you can request removal of those contributed photos by email. Send the request to drinkscan.support@gmail.com from the email address tied to your account.

Retention

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Some records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or compliance reasons. Aggregated or anonymized records that no longer identify you may also be retained.
